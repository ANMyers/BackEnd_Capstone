Welcome to Recognition!


Home and friend to anyone who wants to tinker with different algorithms and learn.


Come to learn? You are in the right place!


Here you can learn about different algorithms and upload your own dataset to train and test against to witness for yourself what they can do. Want to get started? Just clicked on Try It. Not sure where to begin? Just click on About.


This site was built with the help of scikit-learn.
If you don't recognitize the name you owe yourself a favor by checking them out.


Scikit-learn is an amazing resource for beginners and mathematicians alike. They offer a beautiful library of different algorithms at your disposal. Click on the link above to go check them out!
